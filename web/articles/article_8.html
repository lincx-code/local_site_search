Persona 5

The Persona series has always been known for its slick presentation, effortlessly cool vibe, and killer soundtrack. And that’s all without even mentioning the gripping story and memorable cast. Persona 5 follows the students of the fictional Shujin Academy as they navigate double lives as student who become the altruistic “Phantom Thieves” by night, doing their part to rid the world of individuals with corrupt “mind palaces,” or the bad actors who take on villainous personas of their own in the real world.

Offering a combination of dungeon exploration with turn-based battles that work in tandem with visual novel and dating-sim like elements, it makes for one of the wildest, most gripping experiences PlayStation 4 has to offer—and this is only the fifth main entry in the Persona series. Just imagine where it could go from here.
